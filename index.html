Immersion Day

BBD Software hosts an annual Immersion Day, a unique opportunity for students and aspiring developers to gain an insider’s view of the company. This event offers participants a chance to engage with our team of experienced developers, learn about the innovative projects we're working on, and explore the vibrant culture at BBD. Through interactive sessions, hands-on workshops, and insightful discussions, attendees will get a comprehensive understanding of what it's like to work in a dynamic software development environment. Join us for a day of learning, networking, and inspiration as we open our doors to the next generation of tech talent.
